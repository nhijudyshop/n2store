Connect Pancake with Platforms > Activate Page 
After connecting to Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, or in certain situations, you will need to activate the page.
1. Pending Activation Connection
If you don't find your connection on the dashboard after connecting, it may be waiting for activation. You need to activate the page to start using it.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 15.07.38.png
Connection is currently awaiting activation
Select the desired pages from the list, then click to "Activate" button to enable their use. To activate multiple connections at once, select all applicable pages before proceeding.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 15.03.56.png
Depending on the amount of data on your connection, the activation process may take a few minutes.
2. Hidden connection
If you have previously hidden a connection from the dashboard, you can reactivate it from the "Hidden Connection" tab.
