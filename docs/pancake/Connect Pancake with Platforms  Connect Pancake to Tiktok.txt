On Pancake, in order to connect with TikTok Account, we have 3 options TikTok Business Messaging and TikTok Livestream AIO.
1. TikTok Business Messaging
1.1 What is TikTok Business?
TikTok Business is an upgraded account type converted from a regular (personal) TikTok account. It is designed for individuals or organizations engaging in business activities. With a TikTok Business account, you are eligible to run TikTok Messaging Ads.
1.2 What is the TikTok Business Messaging API?
TikTok Business Messaging API is a tool that allows businesses to manage their TikTok Direct Messages through the Pancake platform.
Pancake is currently the exclusive partner in Vietnam and one of the global partners of TikTok Business Messaging.
Through Pancake, TikTok users can easily manage conversations across their TikTok Business accounts in one centralized place.
- A regular TikTok account and a TikTok Business account are not significantly different, and there is no fee for switching to a Business account.
 
- A TikTok Business account allows you to run Click-to-Message ads — a feature that is not available for regular accounts.
