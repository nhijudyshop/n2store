Introduction
Pancake is a software platform within the Pancake ecosystem that supports multi-channel management of messages, comments, sales, and more.
(It integrates with Facebook, Zalo, Instagram, Shopee, Lazada, TikTok, WhatsApp, Line, Tokopedia, and others.)
What is Pancake Software?
Pancake is a multi-channel conversation management software within the Pancake ecosystem. It helps businesses manage messages, comments, and sales across multiple platforms, including Facebook, Zalo, Instagram, Shopee, Lazada, TikTok, WhatsApp, Line, Tokopedia, and more.
Key Features of Pancake
1. Connects to Multiple Platforms
Whether you sell on Facebook, Instagram, Shopee, TikTok, WhatsApp, or Zalo, Pancake integrates with most platforms available today, allowing seamless business management across channels.

3. Smart Conversation Assignment
With auto-routing features, Pancake helps businesses distribute conversations to team members efficiently. Customize assignment rules based on rotation, workload, employee availability, and working hours.
4. Comprehensive and Accurate Report
Pancake provides detailed analytics for each channel and page, helping you track business growth and team performance. Additional advanced reports are also available.
5. Versatile Ecosystem
Pancake’s ecosystem is designed to support diverse business needs with a range of powerful tools and integrations.
