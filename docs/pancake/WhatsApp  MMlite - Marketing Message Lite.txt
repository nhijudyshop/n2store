WhatsApp > MMlite - Marketing Message Lite
MM API for WhatsApp is our next-generation marketing solution built to enhance the customer experience and deliver the right message to more of the right people.
Marketing Messages API for WhatsApp (formerly known as Marketing Messages Lite API) is now generally available.

Upgrade easily, with consistent reliability and security: 
MM API for WhatsApp is easy-to-use, reliable, and secure. It offers a similar technical schema and same billing model as Cloud API, and businesses can use existing phone numbers and MM templates.
Onboarding to MMlite
Step 1: Accept the Terms of Service
You'll need someone with full control of the business portfolio (BM) to accept the Terms of Service and onboard MM API for WhatsApp via WhatsApp Manager.
