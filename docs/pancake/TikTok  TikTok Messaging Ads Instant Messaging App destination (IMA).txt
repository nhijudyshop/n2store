Pancake x TikTok: New Solution for Measuring & Managing Customers from TikTok Message Ads

Pancake and TikTok introduce a new solution to help businesses track and manage customers from TikTok Message Ads across platforms like Messenger and WhatsApp (IMA - Instant Messaging App).

Learn more: https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/how-to-set-up-the-tiktok-integration-on-pancake
1. What are TikTok Messaging Ads to Facebook Messenger & WhatsApp Business?
TikTok recently introduced a new TikTok Messaging Ads objective, allowing businesses to drive customers from TikTok Ads to Messenger or WhatsApp Business as a destination.

3. Setup on Pancake
The instructions below will help you identify customers chatting with you (on WhatsApp Business/Messenger on Pancake) come from TikTok Messaging Ads.
Before start: 
You need a Facebook Page/ WhatsApp Business API  number integrated with Pancake to manage messages. 
You need to have a TikTok advertising account with the Instant Messaging Apps feature enabled.
Login to . Choose your Facebook Page or WhatsApp Business API connection.
IMA only supports WhatsApp Business API. On Pancake, WhatsApp Business API connection starts with prefix waba_
Example: https://pancake.vn/waba_6287711890788
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/1/20/image08075d69-931c-424b-959a-f28cca9dde22.png
WhatsApp Business API connection starts with prefix waba_
	•	Select Setting > Tools > Connect
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/1/20/imagefc49776a-a4fc-4cf8-9305-cbd1d8858171.png
	•	Check the box, then click Authorize
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/1/20/Screenshot 2025-01-20 at 16.51.18.png
	•	Click Confirm to provide permission for Pancake to send the events and get your ad preview
You have to login to TikTok Advertiser Account first to see the screen below. If you don't have a TikTok Ads account, sign-up here
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/1/20/Screenshot 2025-01-20 at 16.52.22.png
	•	Choose your TikTok Ads Accounts you will use to run TikTok Messaging Ads with Instant Messaging App (IMA) as an Optimization location
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/1/20/Screenshot 2025-01-20 at 16.56.51.png
	•	Click Continue to complete the connection steps. You will see the status Connected. That means you just connected Pancake and your TikTok Ads account successfully.
You can copy the WhatsApp phone number in Pancake to put in TikTok Ads IMA campaign later. Refer to image below

6. FAQ
How do I filter conversations that come from a specific Ad ID?
You can filter by:
	•	Select Filter conversations by sources
	•	Choose the tab Filter by Ad ID
	•	Put or choose your specific ad ID then click Filter
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/1/23/Screenshot 2025-01-23 at 16.24.22.png
How do I filter order that come from a specific Ad ID?
On POS, for orders on Facebook Messenger/WhatsApp but originating from TikTok Messaging Ads, you can filter them by:
	•	Accessing Pancake POS and selecting the Shop with the relevant page.
	•	Navigating to the Orders section on the left.
	•	Using the Filter option, adding Ad Source > TikTok.
Orders from TikTok ads will then be listed.
