Review 
View customer feedback statistics for staff members through automatic evaluation messages sent by Botcake. You can review, print, or export evaluation reports to Excel files for analysis and storage.
Note:
- If the system can identify the person who last messaged the customer, the evaluation will be automatically assigned to that person. 
- If the system cannot identify anyone (for example, the customer sends an evaluation by themselves, or no one has replied yet), the evaluation will not be assigned to any staff member.
1. Date range
To view data according to your needs, you must select the desired time range.
Here, you can set the system to display detailed statistics by hour, day, week, month, or year depending on your needs and the selected time period.

2. Analytics charts
After selecting a time range, you will see visual charts showing Evaluations and Top staff with the most evaluations. These help you quickly understand the evaluation status.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2024/8/26/imagea9e40738-eb4c-4710-b80c-7cb628a51910?url=https%3A%2F%2F4078023836-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FYnoXIEwev8YlAw6px2sv%252Fuploads%252F1h26tebslUwJ4YD0Hehn%252FScreenshot%25202024-07-30%2520at%252016.51.57.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df3a4ec2c-1ba4-4fa6-bc5d-ec6a03cd1992&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=9c6d2ad2&sv=1
🔹 Review: Shows the total number of reviews and the average score for each staff member. Evaluation levels include: Good (5 stars), Neutral (3–4 stars), and Needs Improvement (1–2 stars).
	•	Công thức tính :  Average = Total Stars from Customers / Total Number of Review
🔹 Top rated user: Shows the staff members who received the most evaluations. Unassigned evaluations (no specific staff) will not be counted in this list.
🧠 Example: The chart shows Evaluations and Top Staff for this month (current date 27/04/2025):
	•	Good: 800 (times)
	•	Neutral: 1000 (times)
	•	Need Improvement: 200 (times)
🔍 For a full explanation of each metric, please see the Detailed Statistics section below.
