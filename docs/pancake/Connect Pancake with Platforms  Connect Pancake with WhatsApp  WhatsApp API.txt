Connect Pancake with Platforms > Connect Pancake with WhatsApp > WhatsApp API
Requirements to connect and use the WhatsApp API:
✅ Your Business Manager (BM) account must be verified: If it's not verified, the number of daily conversations will be limited.
✅ The WhatsApp number must not be connected to a personal WhatsApp account: If the number was previously registered with regular WhatsApp, you’ll need to delete that account and wait for 3-5 mins before connecting it to the WhatsApp API.
✅ The account used to register for the WhatsApp API must have ADMIN access to the Business Manager.

1️⃣  Create new WhatsApp Business Account
Select the “Create new WhatsApp Business Account" then click "Next"
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/11/19/31e85723-6ee8-4080-861f-2df9797d15a9_Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 16.39.48.png
Fill your business information
WhatsApp Business Account Name: This is your internal WhatsApp name that appears in Business Manager to help you differentiate between accounts. It will not be visible to your customers. But you should put the similar name as the display name (This is the name shown on your WhatsApp profile — it's what your customers will see.)
Category: Choose the product or service category that best matches your business.
Website: Put your ACCESSIBLE website address. It's better if the domain name include your brand name.
Please note that WEBSITE is very important. You should fill the eligible - accessible address. If you don't have website, you can put your business Facebook Page link or the Instagram link. Example: 
https://www.facebook.com/Pancake.vn
https://www.instagram.com/pancakephilippines/
There are also additional (optional) fields such as Time zone, Business description.
Click Next
