Connect Pancake with Platforms > Connect Pancake to Thread
Connecting Threads with Pancake allows you to automatically sync comments, manage them centrally, easily categorize interactions, and respond quickly — ensuring that no engagement is missed.
1. What is Threads?
Threads is a social media platform developed by Meta (Facebook/Instagram) that allows users to post short updates, images, videos, and interact through comments. Threads is closely integrated with Instagram accounts.
When a customer comments on your post, Pancake will automatically collect and display that comment as a conversation. You can manage, respond, and take care of customer interactions all in one place.
2. Why Connect Pancake with Threads?
✅ Automatically sync posts and comments (including images, text, attachments, etc.) from Threads to Pancake.
✅ Help your team respond to customer comments quickly.
✅ Minimize missed orders and customer interactions.
3. Steps to Connect Pancake with Threads
Step 1: Go to the Connect section
From the Dashboard, select Connect.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/8/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-08 lúc 08.51.58.png
Step 2: Select the Threads platform
In the popup window, click the Threads icon → then click the “Connect with Threads” button.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/8/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-08 lúc 09.31.59.png
Step 3: Log in to your Threads account
At this step, the system will display one of the following two scenarios:
Case 1: You are already logged into Threads
In this case, the system will automatically detect and link to your existing account.
Click “Continue as ...” to complete the connection.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/3/iScreen%20Shoter%20-%20Google%20Chrome%20-%20250703145124.jpg
Case 2: You are not logged into Threads
In this case, select the option to log in using your Instagram account that is linked to Threads.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/3/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250703150259.jpg
Then, click “Continue as ...” to complete the login process.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/3/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250703145124.jpg
Step 4: Complete the Connection
Once the connection is successful, your Threads page will appear in the Pancake interface as shown in the image below.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/3/Screenshot 2025-07-03 at 18.08.25.png
From here, you can start monitoring and responding to comments on your Threads posts directly within Pancake.
When a Threads account connects to Pancake for the first time, the system will only fetch posts from the last 14 days. 

*Note: For posts older than 14 days, if there are new comments, the system will automatically update and fetch those comments into Pancake.
