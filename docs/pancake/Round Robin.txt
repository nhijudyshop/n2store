Round Robin
Instructions and explanation of settings related to Round Robin mode
What is Pancake's Round Robin mode?
Pancake’s Round Robin mode allows you to configure conditions for assigning conversations to staff. The assignment can be either manual or automatic, depending on the workflow your business uses.
Only accounts with Admin rights or those granted Round Robin access can use this feature.
Methods of assigning conversations in Round Robin mode

Disable Round Robin Mode
When Round Robin mode is turned off, Admins will see all conversations, and staff will be able to view and respond to all conversations.
The option "Staff can only view conversations assigned to them" (when enabled) will restrict staff to viewing only the conversations assigned to them. They will not be able to see conversations assigned to other staff or unassigned ones. This feature is suitable for pages where Admins prefer to manually assign conversations to staff.
Staff Self-Assignment
This feature allows staff to assign conversations to themselves without needing Admin approval. Staff can proactively take on conversations they are best suited to handle.

In the Conversation interface, staff can assign customers to themselves by clicking the "Assign Conversation" button, as shown in the example below.

Staff can only view conversations assigned to them or unassigned conversations
When this option is enabled, staff can only view and reply to conversations that are either unassigned or have been assigned to them. This helps prevent staff from responding to conversations assigned to others.
Automatically assign to the first staff who replies
With this feature enabled, the system will automatically assign the conversation to the staff member who first replies to the customer — no need to click the "Assign" button. If the staff member does not reply, they will not be assigned the conversation.
Group Assignment
The system will assign conversations based on staff groups, regardless of individual online status, working hours, etc.
Staff within the same assignment group can view and respond to the same conversation.
This feature is ideal for businesses with multiple staff teams working in shifts.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.17.49.png
After creating a group, select staff members for that group on the right-hand side.
Accounts (staff) not selected in the list above will be able to view all conversations on the page.
If this option is enabled, staff who are not part of any group will be able to view and reply to all conversations.
Set conversation distribution ratio
You can configure different distribution ratios for each group. For example, for a group of senior or more experienced staff, you can assign a higher ratio so they receive more conversations.
Click the Group Ratio tab to switch to the conversation distribution settings for staff.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 09.54.47.png
When you change the distribution ratio, the system will continue assigning conversations based on the old ratio until the current cycle is completed. The new ratio will only take effect in the next cycle. You can also click the broom icon to immediately apply the newly updated ratio.
 
Click the number 1 (next to the broom icon) to reset all distribution ratios back to 1.
Select Accounts (Automatic Assignment by System)
This option allows the system to assign conversations to the selected staff accounts in the list.
The assignment logic will follow the conditions configured in the Detailed Configuration section.

Account List
Staff
This is the list of staff members who currently have access to your page. From this list, you can choose who will be assigned message conversations and who will be assigned comment conversations.
The staff members you select first from the list will be prioritized to receive conversations from the system before others.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 09.58.28.png
The green icon at the top right corner of a staff member’s profile picture indicates whether they are currently online on Pancake. This status affects the conversation assignment process if you choose the assignment method as Online Only or Prioritize Online.
The group of icons on the right indicates the conversation receiving status of each staff member. Admins can manually change this status. The statuses include:
	•	Available: Ready to receive conversations
	•	– This staff member is willing to receive new conversations.
	•	– The staff can switch their own status to Away.
	•	– Admins can switch the status to Away or Lock.
	•	Away: Not accepting new conversations
	•	– This staff member does not want to receive new conversations.
	•	– The staff can switch their own status back to Available.
	•	– Admins can switch the status to Available or Lock.
	•	Lock: This status is visible only to Admins.
	•	– When activated, the staff member will not receive new conversations and cannot change their own status to Available or Away.

Staff Ratio
You can set the conversation assignment ratio for each staff member. For example, more experienced staff can be given a higher ratio than new staff.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.00.02.png
The “1” button allows you to quickly reset all assignment ratios to the default value of 1.
You still need to click Save Settings for the system to apply the changes.
The “broom” icon allows you to quickly clear the current assignment queue if the system has not yet completed the cycle based on the old ratio.
If you change the assignment ratio for staff and want the new ratio to take effect immediately, you must check the option "I want to apply the new ratio immediately" or click the broom icon in the top right corner to clear the assignment queue.
 
Example: 
The current message assignment ratio is 3 - 2 - 1. The system requires 3+2+1 = 6 new message assignments to complete one full distribution cycle. However, if only 4 assignments have been made and you change the ratio to 1 - 1 - 1, the system will still complete the remaining 2 assignments based on the old ratio unless you choose Apply the new ratio immediately or click the broom icon. Only after finishing the current cycle will the new 1 - 1 - 1 ratio be applied.
Detailed Configuration
After selecting the staff members to receive conversations and setting the assignment ratios, you can further configure the specific conditions for assigning conversations to those staff.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.01.38.png
Conversation Assignment Method
	•	Even Distribution: The system will always assign conversations evenly to the selected staff members in the list, regardless of whether they are online or not. Each staff member will only see conversations assigned to them. They cannot view or reply to conversations that are unassigned or assigned to others.
	•	Online Only: The system will assign conversations only when there is at least one online staff member. If no staff are online, the system will wait until someone comes online before continuing the assignment.
	•	Prioritize Online:
	•	The system will prioritize assigning conversations to online staff selected in the account list above. If no one is online, it will distribute conversations evenly among the selected staff members.
Shuffle Staff List
When starting a new conversation assignment cycle, the system will automatically shuffle the selected staff list.
This option is commonly enabled when you manage multiple pages with the same staff list. Without shuffling, the first staff in the list may consistently receive more conversations, which can lead to overload.
When Shuffle is enabled, the system randomly selects a starting staff member for each new cycle, reducing the chance that all messages are assigned to the same person.
Number of Main Assignees per Conversation
This is the number of primary staff members who remain assigned to a conversation after additional assignments are made.
For example, if you set 1 main assignee per conversation, there will always be one fixed staff member responsible for that conversation. Additional staff can still be assigned to assist based on your additional assignment rules.
If a conversation has been assigned to a staff member but remains unread after X minutes (X is the configured duration): 
- By default, the system will assign one additional staff member if the conversation is still unread. 
- If the setting “Retry if the conversation is still unread Y times” is enabled, then after the first additional assignment, if the conversation remains unread after X minutes, the system will retry by assigning additional staff Y more times (Y is the configured number of retries).
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.03.17.png
If you set 0 main assignees per conversation, the system will immediately transfer the conversation to another staff member without keeping any main assignee.
The way the system transfers conversations will depend on the rules you have configured.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.05.03.png
Maximum Number of Unread Conversations per Account
When this setting is enabled, each staff member will only receive up to the configured number of unread new conversations. If this number is exceeded, the system will stop assigning new customers to that staff member. (Note: Existing customers who message again will still be assigned to their previous staff, regardless of this limit.)
The system will wait until the number of unread conversations drops below the maximum before assigning new ones.
However, if a customer who previously chatted with Lam Lam sends a new message, the system will still prioritize assigning that customer to Lam Lam, regardless of the unread conversation limit.
Conversation Assignment Time
Based on the working hours configured in General Settings, you can choose whether the system should assign conversations at all times or only during working hours.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.06.23.png
In case you set to assign conversations only during working hours:
	•	During working hours, the system will assign conversations according to other configuration settings as usual.
	•	Outside working hours, the system will by default either evenly assign conversations to the selected staff list, or only assign to online accounts (the system will only assign to online accounts; if no staff are online, the conversation will be placed in the pending assignment list).
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/31/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-31 lúc 10.08.53.png
Viewing Permissions
Just like other options, you can configure conversation viewing permissions for staff members.
Admins can always view all conversations.
	•	For staff not selected in the conversation assignment list: By default, these staff members will not be able to view any conversations. However, you can allow them to view all conversations by enabling the option. In this case, the staff member will be able to see all conversations on the page.
	•	You can also restrict the staff to only view conversations assigned to them by selecting Assigned to me.
	•	If you do not configure the Maximum number of unread conversations per account, you can also allow staff to view conversations assigned to them plus those that have not been assigned to anyone yet.

When not assigned new conversations, staff can still reply to previous conversations that were already assigned to them.
