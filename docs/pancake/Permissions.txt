Instructions for Managing Staff Permissions on Pancake
Note: 
- You can only assign permissions to others if you are an Admin. Facebook and Instagram (connected via your Facebook login) do not support assigning permissions through this method. 
- You must assign permissions directly on the Facebook Page. 
- If you connect Instagram directly to Pancake, you can assign permissions directly within Pancake.
What is Staff Permission in Pancake?
Staff permission in Pancake allows you to manage each staff member’s access to specific pages or features within the system.
You can edit a staff member’s permissions by ticking or unticking the corresponding options for that staff.
The "Add New" button is not displayed for Facebook pages because you need to assign staff permissions directly on Facebook.

Permission Groups in Pancake
Pancake divides permission levels into 3 main groups: Admin, Editor, Moderator, Revoked (this group includes staff who have temporarily lost access to the page)
Feature Groups in Pancake
Corresponding to the permission groups, Pancake also allows you to grant access to different feature groups, including:
	•	Posts: Access to the Posts section and the ability to download phone numbers or download comments
	•	Backup: Access to the backup feature for messages, calls, etc.
	•	Settings: Access to key settings. If you grant Round Robin mode access to a staff member, they will have Admin-level permissions.
	•	Statistics: Access to the entire statistics section
	•	Media & Files: Access to syncing and managing uploaded files

How to Assign Staff Permissions on Pancake
This guide shows you how to assign permissions on Pancake for Facebook and other platforms below.
1. How to Assign Facebook Permissions on Pancake
For Facebook, you need to assign permissions to staff directly on the Facebook Page first.
After that, the staff member logs into Pancake. At this point, the staff’s permissions on Pancake will automatically match their permissions on Facebook.
Assign Permissions Directly on Facebook
You need to select the specific permissions to assign to each staff member.
The Full Control permission on Facebook corresponds to the Admin role on Pancake.
After sending the invitation successfully, the staff member needs to access the page link to accept page management.
Assigning Permissions via Business Manager (BM)
You need to select the specific permissions to assign to each staff member. The Full Control permission corresponds to the Admin role on Pancake.
After sending the invitation successfully, the staff member needs to access their email to accept page management via Business Manager (BM).
2. For other platforms: TikTok, WhatsApp, Shopee, etc.
For platforms other than Facebook, such as WhatsApp, TikTok, Instagram — after successfully connecting them to Pancake and having Admin rights — you can assign permissions for staff to access Pancake features.
Add New Staff
	•	Make sure you have Admin rights.
	•	Access the page where you want to assign permissions.
	•	Go to Settings => Permissions
	•	Select Add New.

In the pop-up window, there are 2 ways to add users to the page as follows:
Method 1: Add users directly on Pancake.
This method requires the person you want to add to have already logged into Pancake.
Method 2: Add by generating a connection code. Send this link to the staff member. After logging in, the staff just needs to click the link and will automatically be assigned the correct role you selected.

To remove a staff member, hover over the pencil icon next to their name and select Remove this person.
