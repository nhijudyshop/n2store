Export
This section helps you track the number of Messages from conversations, Conversations from Ads, Calls, and recordings, and Customer information.
Steps to export Data:
Before viewing statistics, you need to select the Data Source to back up (Messages from conversations, Conversations from Ads, Calls and recordings, Customer information) and the Time Range. This helps you filter the data accurately based on your specific goals.

✅ Step 1: Select "Source" to export
First, choose the type of data you want to back up:
	•	Export messages: Includes conversations from Comments, Inbox, Reviews, Page messages, and phone numbers.
	•	Download conversation from the ads: Includes chats generated through advertising campaigns.
	•	Download call logs: Outbound, inbound, missed calls.
	•	Customer information: Customer tags, Ad IDs, and notes.
Once selected, you can either download the statistics or view your backup history.
✅ Step 2: Select "Time Range"
After choosing the content to back up, select a time range to filter the data.
You can customize the time range by hour, day, week, month, or year based on your needs.
 The selected time range in the "Overview" section may differ from the one in the "Export" section. Please double-check to ensure you're viewing the correct data.
If you choose Download, click "Create Request" to start the backup.
✅ Step 3: Choose File Format
You can choose to download the backup in Excel format or apply filters and export it as a TXT file.
	•	Excel: You can further filter data using conversation tags and export options.
	•	TXT filters: This option filters out duplicate phone numbers.
Exporting your data helps protect important information and allows easy access and analysis of conversations, calls, and customer information, helping you optimize work performance efficiently.
