Setting Up Messenger Calls on Pancake
✅ Step 1: Enable Messenger Calls
Access your Page on Pancake. Go to Page Settings > Calls > In the Call Feature Settings row, select Pancake.
✅ Step 2: Advanced Settings
This section allows you to select the Facebook Page where you want to enable the calling feature and customize the following settings:
Show call button on the buyer's interface: Toggle the visibility of the call button for your Page on the buyer's Messenger app interface.
Call reception schedule: Customize the timeframe (by day of the week) when you are available to receive calls. Click on a blank slot on the calendar and drag to create the time blocks during which customers can reach you.
Ring for incoming calls: Set up priority ringing for the staff member assigned to the corresponding conversation. There are two options: Prioritize assigned staff (rings first for the assigned staff; if offline or busy, rings for all online staff) or All staff (rings for all online staff immediately).
Auto-tag conversations: After a call ends, the system will automatically apply tags — select the tag for after a successful call and the tag for after a missed call.

3. Making Outgoing Calls to Customers on Facebook via Pancake
The guide below will show you how to call customers who have previously contacted your Facebook Page, right from Pancake.
💡 Scenario 1: The customer called your Facebook Page first
In the message input box, click the Call button. Then, depending on your Call Settings:
If you select Call Customer → You can call them immediately and maintain the right to call for 7 days without sending a “Call Request” message.
If you select Request a Call → The system will send a message asking for permission to call (similar to Scenario 2).
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/7/Screenshot 2025-03-07 at 16.35.38.png
When the customer calls first
💡 Scenario 2: The customer has not called your Facebook Page yet
In the message input box, click the Call button to send a Call Request. The customer will receive a message from your Page asking them to Allow or Not Allow you to call them.
If the customer Allows the call, please note:
If you select Call Customer → You can call them immediately and maintain the right to call for 7 days without sending a “Call Request” message.
If you select Request a Call → You will be required to send another permission message before calling.

💡 Scenario 3: The customer clicked on an ad and requested a callback
In this case, you will be able to make an outgoing call to the customer immediately, similar to Scenario 1 above.

Note:
- You can send a call request up to 2 times per customer per day.
- Call requests can only be sent within 24 hours of the customer’s last message.

4. Frequently Asked Questions
 1. When will my staff receive incoming calls?
Staff members must have Pancake open and be online to be eligible to receive calls. Incoming calls will be prioritized to display and ring for the staff member assigned to that specific conversation. If the conversation is unassigned, the call will ring for all online staff members.
 2. Can a staff member handle two calls at the same time?
No. Each staff member can only connect to one call at a time. The current call must be ended before another call can be connected.
