Connection
The "Connection" section in Pancake helps you follow customer engagement and sales performance on your Facebook page. This enables you to make informed decisions to enhance customer service and boost business efficiency.
1. Date range
Before viewing the data, you need to select the time range (by hour, day, week, month, or year). This helps filter the data according to your specific analysis goals.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/4/27/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250427170701.jpg
🧠 Example: The statistics in the Page will be filtered for this month, specifically from April 1, 2025, to April 27, 2025.
The time frame selected in the "Overview" section may differ from that in the "Connection" section. Please double-check to ensure you're viewing the correct data.
2. Overview
This section gives you an overview of the interaction activity on your page, displayed through charts and growth metrics based on the time range you’ve chosen.
Displayed Content:
	•	A chart shows the number of customer comments and messages sent to your page over specific intervals.
	•	Key metrics include: New phone numbers, Re-engagement via messaging, New customers, and New message conversations.
These metrics help you quickly understand interaction trends on your page, allowing you to assess the effectiveness of your activities during specific periods.
