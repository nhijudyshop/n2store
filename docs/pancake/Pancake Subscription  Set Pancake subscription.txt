Pancake Subscription > Set Pancake subscription
You can easily set up your Pancake subscription by adding connections, adding users, or sharing your subscription.
Connections and users must be in the same subscription to use Pancake services.
1. Subscription Settings Screen
	•	Step 1. From your Pancake account information page, next to the subscription status column, select Subscription Settings

Pancake account information screen
1. The settings screen provides information about:
	•	Connections: The number of connections added and allowed to use your subscription.
	•	Users: The number of users added and allowed to use your subscription.
	•	Price: The subscription fee.
	•	Payment Date: The activation and payment date.
	•	Expiration: The end date of the subscription term.
	•	Auto config: Automatically add pages to your subscription.

2. Adding/Removing Connections in a Subscription
To add or remove a page in your Pancake subscription:
	•	Step 1. Access the settings screen of a specific subscription.
	•	Step 2. Select the Connections tab.
	•	Step 3. Check or uncheck connections to add or remove them.
	•	Step 4. Click Save to save your changes.
You must have permission for the connection to add it to your subscription. Use search and filters to find connections you can manage.

3. Adding/Removing Users in a Subscription
To add or remove users:
	•	Step 1.Access the settings screen for a specific subscription.
	•	Step 2. Select the Users tab.
	•	Step 3. Check or uncheck users to add or remove them.
	•	Step 4. Click Save to save your changes.

4. Sharing or Unsharing Your Subscription
4.1 To share your Pancake subscription with others:
	•	Step 1. Access the settings screen of a specific subscription.
	•	Step 2. Select the Sharing tab.
	•	Step 3. Search for and select the person you want to share with (by name, email, or Facebook ID).

Share Pancake subscription
4.2 To unshared your subscription:
	•	Step 1. Access the settings of a specific subscription.
	•	Step 2. Select the Sharing tab.
	•	Step 3. Search for and select the user to unshared.
	•	Step 4. Click the Delete icon.
	•	Step 5. Click OK to remove them from your subscription.
