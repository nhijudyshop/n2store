WhatsApp > WhatsApp API entry points
Entry points are gateways to WhatsApp conversations. There are many ways to lead your customers to your WhatsApp number and engage in a conversation.
1. Whatsapp Discover Businesses
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/2/11/AD_4nXcL41q9hAIKdft9M5aMV-kYkBrg2RBIQfSJ09yrCdPAaK_axhvIVXV40EJuuUjxsJlIXRe3sgKCAHP8Zx7PnqTfTrt1r1fK0e00eZSeAY60Zo9mWbN7nAKYAwye0OHN_E4gooOm2w?key=LBMeE69SFxQglr1AIQHvHdqA
The most simple way to guide your customer to chat with you through WhatsApp is the Discover Businesses search. Customers can directly search for your business within the app.
Business search results will match in full or in part the text the user has entered. When you search for businesses, search terms are processed in a de-identified way that will not be linked back to your account. For example, when searching for a bakery in your area, search terms such as “bakeries near me”, “cake”, or “custom cupcakes” will not be associated with your WhatsApp account or phone number.
The results displayed will only show businesses registered in your customer's country. For example, if your phone number starts with +44 for the UK, they will only see results with the same country code.
See more about WhatsApp Discover Businesses

3. QR code
A QR code can serve as a good entry point on your printed receipt, products, or simply on the promotion picture.

Once you generated a WhatsApp link in section two, search for a QR code generator and create it. When the customer scans it with their phone, they will be redirected to WhatsApp.
4. Paid entry points: Click to WhatsApp Ads (CTWA)
While setting up the ads, you can choose the destination for the Call-to-action button. When the client clicks on the button, they will be redirected directly to your business' WhatsApp number.

Conversations initiated from these ads won't incur charges for the first 72 hours, regardless of the type of template or conversation, if you respond within 24 hours.
If you do not respond within 24 hours, a free entry point conversation is not opened and you must use a template to message the customer, which opens a marketing, utility, or authentication conversation, per the category of the template.

 After this free 72h period, regular conversation fees apply. Each conversation originating from click-to-WhatsApp ads will have a unique Advertisement ID and can be tailored to automate responses based on each ad.
