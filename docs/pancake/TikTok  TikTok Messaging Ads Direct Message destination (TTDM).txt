Pancake x TikTok: New Solution for Measuring & Managing Customers from TikTok Message Ads

Pancake and TikTok introduce a new solution to help businesses track and manage customers who click on TikTok Message Ads, then start a conversation with a TikTok Business Account via Direct Message.
1. What is TikTok Messaging Ads to TikTok Direct Message?
TikTok Messaging Ads are a new type of ad on TikTok. With this ad, customers can tap the message button on a TikTok video to start a chat with your business.
2. What are TikTok Messaging Ads to TikTok Direct Messages?
TikTok recently introduced a new TikTok Messaging Ads objective, allowing businesses to drive customers from TikTok Ads to TikTok Direct Message (TikTok inbox)
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/7/Screenshot%202025-03-07%20at%2017.31.32.png
3. Why Should You Use Pancake Together with This Type of Ad?
When using this type of ad with Pancake, you can manage conversations easily, quickly, and effectively — even if you run many channels and many ads. More importantly, when a customer places an order or when there is spam, Pancake will send signals back to TikTok to automatically optimize your ads.
We can point out some reasons why Pancake is very important for the advertiser when using this type of ad. Details below:
Ads Optimization
Pancake and TikTok have a deep-funnel events to auto-optimized for your ads once your TikTok Ad account connected to Pancake

4. Setup on Pancake
The instructions below will guide you through setting up the connection between Pancake and TikTok, including linking your TikTok Ads account to receive optimized advertising signals from Pancake.
⚠️ Before you start, make sure that: 
- You have successfully connected your TikTok Business Messaging channel to Pancake. If not, please check the guide here. 
- You are already using Pancake to reply to customers on TikTok Business Messaging.
- You have a TikTok Ads account with the TikTok Direct Message/ Tin nhắn trực tiếp trên Tiktok enabled (highlighted in red in the image below).
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/7/Screenshot%202025-03-07%20at%2017.17.54.png
You need to authorize + set up Pancake to access your TikTok Ads account. This is required so Pancake can: 
- Show the correct TikTok Ads linked to your customer conversations. 
- Send optimization events back to TikTok to improve your ad performance.
	•	✅ Step 1: First, log in to  → then go to your TikTok Business Messaging channel.
 For example, in the screenshot below, the channel selected is Linh Chuối nè. 
Note: TikTok Business Messaging channels always start with ttm_.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/7/Screenshot%202025-03-07%20at%2018.03.03.png
	•	✅ Step 2:After opening your TikTok Business Messaging channel, go to Cài đặt/ Setting → Then Công cụ/ Tools → Click Kết nối/ Connect under the TikTok Messaging Ads Settings, as shown in the screenshot below.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2024/8/27/image55724e77-2095-4769-a94d-53ce8f3c1a41?url=https%3A%2F%2F4078023836-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FYnoXIEwev8YlAw6px2sv%252Fuploads%252FqfwugTqwUqlx81hqDguT%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D320141af-daf3-4ddb-8fe3-5f99f3346f89&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=f4fff05&sv=1
	•	✅ Step 3: At this step, you will see the screen below → ClickUỷ quyền/ Authorize to grant Pancake access to your TikTok Ads account.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2024/8/27/image172230ff-e381-4258-8a07-d34f050b9776?url=https%3A%2F%2F4078023836-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FYnoXIEwev8YlAw6px2sv%252Fuploads%252FBAYdatD2oGqs6SV9Y7fx%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df13eb4f5-904f-4ca4-9e4c-a205b403e9d8&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=766b6053&sv=1
	•	✅ Step 4: Next, click Xác nhận/Confirm to grant Pancake access to your TikTok Ads accounts. After confirming, you will be redirected back to Pancake.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/11/Screenshot 2025-03-11 at 12.03.40.png
On this screen, please check if the TikTok account you are about to authorize for Pancake is the one that has permission to manage your Ads account.
- Case 1: If it’s not the correct account, click Switch Account and log in with the right TikTok Ads account.
- Case 2: If it’s correct, click Confirm to authorize.
If your account doesn’t have an Ads account yet, you will see the message below. 
→ In that case, click Tạo tài khoản/ Create Account and then return to Pancake to set up again from the beginning.

	•	✅ Step 5: Next, select the Ads account(s) you want to use for running ads on this TikTok channel. Here, you can choose multiple accounts → then click Continue.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/11/Screenshot 2025-03-11 at 12.06.00.png
	•	✅  Step 6: At this point, the setup on Pancake is complete. You will see a list of Ads accounts that have been connected to your TikTok Business Messaging channel.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/11/Screenshot 2025-03-11 at 12.06.41.png
Each Ads account will correspond to an Eventset – this is where data is stored to track and optimize ad campaigns on TikTok, and it will be used when setting up your campaigns.

5.1 Steps to Create an Ad Campaign
You need to complete the connection steps with Pancake above before starting to create an ad campaign
The video below shows you how to set up an IMA campaign. Please note that you need to pay attention to and choose exactly the following points:
	•	✅ Step 1: In the Ad Objective section/ Mục tiêu quảng cáo, set the conversion goal to: Lead Generation/ Tìm kiếm khách hàng tiềm năng.

	•	✅ Step 2: In the Optimization Location/ Địa điểm tối ưu hoá, select: TikTok Direct Message/ Tin nhắn trực tiếp trên TikTok (highlighted in red in the image below).
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/7/Screenshot%202025-03-07%20at%2017.17.54.png
	•	✅ Step 3: In the Bidding and Optimization section/ Đặt giá thầu và tối ưu hoáselect the Optimization Goal to: Conversation.

At this stage, you’ve finished all the important steps.
For the remaining settings (like ad budget, ad video, etc.), just set them up the same way you normally would. Once done, click Continue/ Tiếp tục to complete your ad setup.

6. How to Read Metrics on TikTok Ads Manager
The guide below will help you track the number of conversations started on Pancake and synced directly to TikTok Ads Manager.
👉 In other words, you’ll be able to see how many customers messaged you through Pancake, and this data will be sent back to TikTok Ads Manager to measure your ad performance.
	•	✅ Step 1: On the TikTok Ads Manager interface, select Default Columns/ Cột mặc định → Custom Columns/ Cột tuỳ chỉnh.
