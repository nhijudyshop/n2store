Instructions for Connecting Pancake with Instagram.
What is Instagram Business?
Instagram Business is the official platform provided by Instagram for professional use. It is commonly referred to as a Professional Account.
If you are currently using a personal Instagram account, you will be guided to switch to a Professional Account in order to connect with Pancake. Alternatively, you can manually change your account type via the Instagram mobile app.
You can see the term "Instagram official" used instead of Instagram Business
Method 1: Connect to Instagram when connecting to Facebook
That measure just applies when you own the Instagram Business and have integrated it to a Facebook Page.
If you connect Instagram to Pancake using this method, you will need to assign permissions to your staff directly on Facebook or Instagram whenever you want to add them. Staff accounts cannot be added, edited, or removed via Pancake.

Method 2: Directly connect Pancake to Instagram
- You may come across the term Instagram Direct, which refers to an Instagram Business account connected directly to Instagram without going through Facebook. 
- If you connect Instagram to Pancake using this method, you will be able to add, edit, or remove staff members who manage the Instagram page directly within Pancake.
	•	✅ Step 1: Log in to Pancake. Choose Connect
