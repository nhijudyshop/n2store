Connect Pancake with Platforms > Connect Pancake with WhatsApp > WhatsApp API Co-Existence
This connection method allows you to use both the WhatsApp Business App and the WhatsApp API simultaneously. As a result, your entire chat history will be synchronized.
1. Overview of WhatsApp API Co-Existence
→ What are the benefits of WhatsApp API Co-Existence?
This connection method allows you to use the WhatsApp Business App on your mobile and Pancake at the same time.
→ Unsupported Regions & Countries
WhatsApp Business App Coexistence is not supported in the following regions and countries:
Regions: European Economic Area (EEA), European Union (EU), United Kingdom (UK)
Countries: Australia, India, Japan, Nigeria, Philippines, Russia, South Korea, South Africa, Turkey

→ So, what do you get from WhatsApp API Co-Existence?
Once you’ve connected your WhatsApp Business App number to the WhatsApp API Co-Existence, you’ll get:
✅ Synced chat history
All messages from your phone will be automatically uploaded to the WhatsApp API so you can stay connected with your customers without interruption.
✅ Full access to WhatsApp API features
Use message templates, bulk messaging, automation flows, auto-replies, buttons, and product catalogs, and only pay when you use these features.
✅ Keep all the features on your WhatsApp Business App
You can still chat with customers for free directly from your phone, just like before.
