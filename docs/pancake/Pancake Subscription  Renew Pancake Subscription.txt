Pancake Subscription > Renew Pancake Subscription
When your subscription is about to expire, you can easily renew it to continue enjoying Pancake’s services.
You can renew your subscription up to 7 days before it expires.
	•	Step 1: Select the subscription you want to renew and click the Renew Subscription icon.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/cc701294-500a-459a-abaa-a89d4a16202f_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 10.00.46.png
Renewing Your Pancake Subscription
Step 2: Enter the required electronic invoice information. After completing this step, click Save and Generate QR Code for payment.
- If you haven't provided electronic invoice info for your current subscription, please update and complete it as required. Select either Individual or Business to create an invoice. 
- You only need to enter this information once; future creation, upgrades, or renewals will not require you to re-enter it.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/d18ab750-3e54-404d-b556-5a9d56b03bdf_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 10.01.52.png
Enter electronic invoice information.
	•	Step 3: Pay for your renewed subscription by scanning the QR code.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/83ac4f6b-17c3-4be0-8fec-e89a55bb1e89_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 10.02.28.png
Pay for your renewed Pancake subscription.
- After your bank transfer is successfully processed, your subscription will be automatically activated within a few minutes. 
- If you enter incorrect information or your subscription is not activated automatically, please contact us

Once payment is complete, your renewed Pancake subscription will be activated.
