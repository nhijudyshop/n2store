Instructions for connecting Pancake with TiktokShop
Requirements:
- You must be the owner of the TikTokShop account. 
For Reference: Guide to Signing Up for TikTok Shop
- TikTok Shop in Indonesia now is Tokopedia x Shop, you can refer the connection guide here
Step 1: On the Pancake homepage, select 'Connect'
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-21 lúc 12.15.34.png
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/16/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-16 lúc 08.48.37.png
Step 4: Enter your information and log in to your TikTok Shop account.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/16/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-16 lúc 08.50.06.png
Step 5: Fill in the configuration/setup details.
You will be asked to enter your email address and phone number (optional) to confirm the connection setup with Pancake.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/16/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-16 lúc 09.04.19.png
Step 6: Grant authorization to Pancake.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/16/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-16 lúc 09.03.43.png
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/16/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-16 lúc 08.51.50.png
Step 7: Connect to the POS system
You will be asked to select your TikTok Shop store and the corresponding warehouse (if your store has multiple warehouses).
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/16/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-16 lúc 08.52.33.png
After a successful connection, the TikTok Shop connection page will appear in the Dashboard.
