Guide to Connecting Pancake with YouTube to Manage Comments and Videos on Your Channel
Check Connection Permissions
Your Gmail account must be the owner of the YouTube channel to connect it with Pancake. Users with the Manager role cannot connect. Follow the steps below to check:
Step 1: Go to the YouTube channel you want to connect to Pancake.
On the YouTube interface, navigate to the channel you wish to connect, then click Customize Channel.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 14.40.24.png
Step 2: Go to Settings and select the Permissions tab.
Here, you will see the Gmail account listed with the Owner role. This is the Gmail account that has the required permissions to connect Pancake with the YouTube channel.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 14.35.05.png
Steps to Connect Pancake with Your YouTube Channel
The following guide will help you connect Pancake with your YouTube channel to manage comments on your videos.
Step 1: Log in to Pancake and go to the Connect section.
Step 2: Select Connect with YouTube.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/8/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-08 lúc 09.30.53.png
Step 3: In the popup window, sign in with the Gmail account that is the Owner of the YouTube channel.
If you own multiple YouTube channels, you can select the desired one during this step. By default, each Gmail account is usually linked to at least one YouTube channel.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 14.44.27.png
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 14.44.37.png
Step 4: Grant Pancake permission to access the selected YouTube channel.
You need to check the option that allows Pancake to:
View, edit, and permanently delete your YouTube videos, ratings, comments, and captions.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/14/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-14 lúc 14.44.50.png
Step 5: Click "Continue" to complete
