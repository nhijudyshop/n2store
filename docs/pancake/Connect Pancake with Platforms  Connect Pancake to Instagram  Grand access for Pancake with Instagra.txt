Connect Pancake with Platforms > Connect Pancake to Instagram > Grand access for Pancake with Instagram-linked FB page (IGO)
To allow Pancake to connect with your Instagram account linked to a Facebook page, you may need to grant access to Pancake through the app.
If you encounter Error 124 when accessing Instagram via Pancake (as shown in the image below), please grant Pancake the necessary access permissions within the Instagram application.

https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2024/10/18/imaged39098e8-881e-4d7a-8647-7e0afa4de744.png
Steps to grant permissions: 
	•	✅  Step 1: Access to Facebook . Change to the Facebook profile that is linked to your Instagram account
	•	✅  Step 2: Access to "Settings & privacy" → "Settings" ()
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/11/27/89d31734-02cd-4980-a80a-88a5cd8b08f7_SCR-20251127-nqqh.png
	•	✅  Step 3: Choose "Linked accounts" → "Instagram" ()
	•	✅  Step 4: Enable "Allow access to Instagram messages in Inbox."
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/11/27/e212d1e3-18a1-49e2-aee0-81352af2a2fd_image 2025-11-27 14-35-01.png
	•	✅  Step 5: Return to Pancake and reaccess this Instagram account.
