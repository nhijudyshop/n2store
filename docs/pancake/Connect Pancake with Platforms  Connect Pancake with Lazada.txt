Connect Pancake with Platforms > Connect Pancake with Lazada
The steps below will show you how to connect Pancake with Lazada
Step 1: Register the Pancake V2 app and Pancake POS app on the Lazada Service Marketplace
Visit Lazada Service Marketplace and register, grant power for 2 Pancake and Pancake applications. These two applications are completely free.
Register Pancake

https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/6/5/SCR-20250605-oaiy.png
Click Authorized Use to grant the Pancake V2 application to manage customer conversations.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/6/5/SCR-20250605-obhb.png
Click Agree and Signed, and click Confirm to register the application.
Register Pancake POS

https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/6/5/SCR-20250605-oatr.png
Press Authorized Use to grant the POS V2 application to manage order, product information.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/6/5/SCR-20250605-obhb.png
Click Agreed and Signed, and click Confirm to register the application.
Step 2: From the Dashboard, select the Connect button. In the Lazada section, choose to connect to the Lazada account
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/6/19/Screenshot 2025-06-19 at 11.27.00.png
Step 3: On the Lazada window, enter the email and password of the Lazada admin seller to finish the connection
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/5/26/Screenshot 2025-05-26 at 17.58.44.png
