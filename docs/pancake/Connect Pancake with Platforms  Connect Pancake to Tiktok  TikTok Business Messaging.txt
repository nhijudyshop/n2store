Guide to Connecting Pancake with TikTok Business for Managing TikTok Direct Messages
Pancake is a global partner of TikTok and one of the first partners integrated with the TikTok Business Messaging API for managing TikTok messages.
What is a TikTok Business Account?
TikTok Business (TikTok Business Account) is a type of account upgraded from a normal TikTok (personal) account, designed for individual entrepreneurs or businesses. With a TikTok Business account, you can use TikTok Messaging Ads
Free Upgrade: Switching from a personal TikTok account to a TikTok Business account does NOT cost anything. 

How to switch to a TikTok Business account: You can see the detailed instructions in [Step 4](https://pke.gg/03woag5e) below.

Messaging Ads: Only TikTok Business accounts can run TikTok Messaging Ads (Click to TikTok Direct Message). If you don't have a TikTok Ads account, sign up here

Check account settings: Make sure your TikTok account is set to allow Messages and Comments from Everyone. Also, turn off all message and comment filters to ensure every message is fully synced with Pancake. You can follow the instructions in the image below to update these settings.\ \ **TikTok’s official guide:* *****

What is the TikTok Business Messaging API?
The TikTok Business Messaging API is a tool that enables businesses to manage TikTok Direct Messages through the Pancake platform.
The following steps will guide you through the login and setup process for TikTok Business Messaging. You will also be guided on how to convert a regular (personal) TikTok account into a TikTok Business account.

Tips: Using Botcake for TikTok Business Messaging automation
Botcake can connect to TikTok for Business accounts and perform actions such as:
Default reply: Ensure custmers always receive a response, even for unexpected questions. You can set a cycle for how often this message should be sent.
Keywords: Define keywords or phrases customers might say. Every time a customer sends a message to the page with content that matches a keyword, Botcake will automatically respond and attach an action to it. For instance, "price" could trigger information on product costs.
Sequences: Sequences are like mini-conversations where the bot sends pre-created messages in a specific order. This is perfect for guiding users through processes like placing orders or booking appointments.
Rules: Set rules based on keywords, user info, or conversation history. If a condition is met, the bot takes action, like sending a specific message or connecting the user to a live agent.
