Pancake Subscription > Upgrade Pancake Subscription
When you need to increase the number of connections or users, you can upgrade your Pancake subscription.
The remaining time on your old subscription will be converted into a specific monetary value and deducted from the price of your new subscription.
	•	Step 1: Select the Upgrade option.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/0a95889a-3b32-427c-8742-d5eecb49e189_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 10.57.58.png
Upgrade Pancake subscription
Step 2: Choose from the suggested subscriptions available or select Create custom plan
- The upgraded subscription must be of higher value than your current subscription. 
- Subscriptions with less than one day remaining cannot be upgraded. Instead of upgrading, you can create a new subscription.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/0eb0a7ec-b159-484d-b1af-e4202e76f3ef_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 10.59.10 1.png
Pancake subscription upgrade options
	•	Step 3: Review your subscription options, then click Create Subscription
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/f7386319-2f6f-4896-82da-1d8a2ec30915_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 11.00.30.png
Review upgraded subscription information
Your previous subscription information is shown above the new options. The remaining value of your current subscription will be credited and subtracted from the price of your new subscription.
Step 4: Enter electronic invoice information as required. Once complete, click Save and get QR code to create a payment code.
- If you haven't provided electronic invoice info for your current subscription, please update and complete it as required. Select either Individual or Business to create an invoice. 
- You only need to enter this information once; future creation, upgrades, or renewals will not require you to re-enter it.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/35d3086a-7fed-48f4-ba7d-a5b3efc8a2c2_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 11.01.40.png
Enter electronic invoice information
	•	Step 5: Pay for your upgraded subscription by scanning the QR code.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/e016365b-4b54-4fef-b39d-35e026a23371_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 11.02.32.png
Pay for your upgraded Pancake subscription
- After your bank transfer is successfully processed, your subscription will be automatically activated within a few minutes. 
- If you enter incorrect information or your subscription is not activated automatically, please contact us
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/a6fab233-a837-400c-82a2-6be861b3b48a_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 10.52.20-Photoroom.png
Payment Successful
Once payment is successful, your upgraded Pancake subscription will be activated.
