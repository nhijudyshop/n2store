WhatsApp > Mass Broadcast / Blasting on WhatsApp API > Regular broadcast
Broadcast WITHOUT variables in the template.
Using WhatsApp API, you can broadcast mass messages to the client's WhatsApp number.
Note: While broadcasting via WhatsApp may seem convenient, it's crucial to adhere to best practices. Your WhatsApp API number may be blocked if clients report or block your broadcasts. Avoid spamming clients, refrain from sending inappropriate content, and always provide an option for clients to unsubscribe from your broadcast stream.
1. Preparation
To do a proper broadcast, please ensure that your WhatsApp number is working properly on Botcake. If you do not see your number appear here, please click the "Add new page" button and connect it.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/24/imagee8dee4c4-9636-496b-9948-943df63009f1.png
Next, ensure that you have created a proper template, and the template is approved 
2. Broadcast away!
To create a broadcast campaign, choose “Broadcasts” on the toolbar → “+ New message”
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/24/imagec01de7ee-510b-45ab-a46b-76fbe3b1b403.png
On the “New messages”, delete the default content and choose “Templates”.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/24/image89d8a12d-31cf-49a8-abcb-b770025144c0.png
Choose the approved template that you prepared from the list.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/24/image9da51ac9-88c4-4e3b-afdc-35f5c6ab1fba.png
Sending templates means opening a new 24h conversation, you’ll be charged once the templates are sent successfully, and the fee depends on the type of your template. More pricing details are here.
Note: With the template which contains an image, you must upload the image again. This one is not necessarily the same as the file approved by Meta.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/24/image56a52859-9a28-4537-81ed-3caf3512a381.png
With buttons, you can set up automatic flows when your customers click them. Each button can be used once only.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/imagecb26a3ca-80d6-43ba-97e9-d162fe16b6ea.png
After carefully rechecking, click “Settings” or “Next step”
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/image528bcf92-fe3a-4d45-a441-ac46c6ebfc10.png
There are three types of messages for you to choose:
	•	The first two options (Messages after 24h and Messages within 24h) will use the customers’ info available in Botcake (who have already interacted with your WhatsApp number). With these two, you can add filter conditions below to send messages only to a group of customers.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/image7f2ffd21-173e-4d6e-8b0e-0e360864a90a.png
	•	With the third option, you can choose to upload an Excel file containing the phone numbers of customers. Botcake will automatically send mass messages to those numbers. Please check carefully before uploading.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/image4127d6cf-9932-4247-9817-092be4187a37.png
If you are only using a simple template without any variation, the file you upload should only contain one column: Phone number.
Please remember to have the country code at the beginning of the number.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/image5d999c7f-6e43-4da1-9abd-224eda26847f.png
You also can schedule time by choosing a date and time.
For the “Number of customers” and “Maximum frequency of sending” sections you can use the default settings.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/imageed702c92-8628-4147-9f48-0bb6bab78e6a.png
Finally, choose “Send now” to send mass messages to your customers.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/3/25/imagee176cf20-b125-4fcd-b11d-5d9db2c7393b.png
