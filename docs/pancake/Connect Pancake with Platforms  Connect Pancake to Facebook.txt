Instructions for Connecting Pancake with Instagram.
Requirement:
 
To connect a Facebook Page to Pancake, you must have the correct permissions on that Page (such as Admin or Standard access with publishing rights). 
If the Page is managed under a Facebook Business Manager, make sure your account has been properly assigned access within that Business Manager.
 
📚 Helpful guides (clear and step-by-step):
 
1️⃣ What is Facebook Business Manager? It’s a tool that helps you manage Pages, ad accounts, and people in one place. 👉 Learn about Business Manager
 
2️⃣ How to create a new Business Manager: 👉 See guide here
 
3️⃣ How to add your Page to Business Manager: If your Page is not yet in Business Manager, you’ll need to add it. 👉 See guide here
 
4️⃣How to assign or change Page roles in Business Manager: If you don’t have the required permissions, an Admin can assign them to you. 👉 See guide here

 5️⃣ How to add people to your Business Manager: If you’re not yet part of the Business Manager, ask an Admin to add your account. 👉 See guide here

✅ Step 1: From the Pancake homepage → Click “Sign In”
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/2/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250702110535.jpg
✅ Step 2: Select the business management processes you are currently managing.
Recommended: Choose "Opt in to all current and future Pages"
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/2/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250702113416.jpg
✅ Step 3: Select the Facebook Pages you want to connect to Pancake.
Recommended: Choose "Opt in to all current and future Pages"
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/2/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250702112228.jpg
✅ Step 4: Confirm Pancake’s access to your Pages
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/2/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250702112305.jpg
At this point, you have successfully connected your Facebook Pages to Pancake.
You will be redirected to Pancake’s dashboard, where the Pages you just connected will now appear.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/2/iScreen Shoter - Google Chrome - 250702112609.jpg
✅ Step 5: Activate the Page
If you don’t see your Facebook Page on the dashboard, please check the connection and activate the Page to start using it. See the guide:
Pancake will sync conversations from the past 14 days.
