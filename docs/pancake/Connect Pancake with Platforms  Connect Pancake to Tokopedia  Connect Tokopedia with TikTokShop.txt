Guide to connecting Tokopedia with TikTokShop
Requirements: 
- You must be the owner of the Tokopedia account.
 - Your Tokopedia account needs to be verified with Gojek and GoPay first.
Step 1: Select “Join your store” to connect with TikTok Shop in your Tokopedia Seller Account
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-21 lúc 16.27.19.png
Step 2: Create or Log in to a Tokopedia & TikTok Shop Seller Account
If you don’t have an account, proceed to register a new seller account for both Tokopedia and TikTok Shop.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-21 lúc 17.16.43.png
If you already have accounts, choose “Log in with existing Tokopedia & TikTok Shop accounts”.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-21 lúc 17.15.56.png
Step 3: Scan the QR Code Using Your Phone
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Ảnh màn hình 2025-07-21 lúc 09.38.54.png
Step 4: Register/Login and Verify Your Account
From this step, you will switch to performing the actions on your phone.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Screenshot_20250721_095458_Seller Center.jpg
Image of the new account registration process
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/IMG_AEEE3EF341FB-1.jpeg
Image of the login process to your account
Once you successfully log into your account, the system will move your account to 
4.1 Enter the OTP sent to your registered phone number.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Screenshot_20250721_095546_Seller Center.jpg
4.2 Grant the requested permissions and review your account information.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Screenshot_20250721_095601_Seller Center.jpg
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/25/image51f7507a-1ac4-4bfe-b8d9-1aabe9f35a2e 2025-07-25 14-48-42.tiff
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/25/image10bbf871-d069-47b8-a713-9fdfe744cc5e 2025-07-25 14-49-03.tiff
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Screenshot_20250721_095722_Seller Center.jpg
Once completed, your account will enter a "review" status.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Screenshot_20250721_095739_Seller Center.jpg
After successful approval, the system will show a confirmation message.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/Screenshot_20250721_135933_Seller Center.jpg
Refresh the Tokopedia Seller page, choose button "check now", and log in your Tokopedia & TikTokShop account again to start managing your store.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/7/21/imagee286078c-e4ef-469b-8168-e46bc0c9c987 2025-07-21 14-55-04.tiff
Step 5 (Optional): Connect Your TikTok Account
Connect your TikTok account with the store management system on Tokopedia & TikTok Shop. This allows you to sync products, manage orders, handle payments, or run ads on TikTok.
5.1 Choose the option to connect your TikTok account.
