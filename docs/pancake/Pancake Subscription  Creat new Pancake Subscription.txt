Pancake Subscription > Creat new Pancake Subscription
How to create a new Pancake subscription according to your needs:
	•	Step 1: Go to the Pancake Account Management page at 
	•	Step 2: Select Create
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/df0eaf7b-f54f-4ef6-96a2-f278817e2455_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 09.11.15.png
On the subscription management screen, click "Create"
	•	Step 3: Choose a suitable pre-made subscription, or select Create custom plan to tailor a plan to your needs.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/6e643849-2617-4af7-b8a0-e64a3cc5bf0c_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 09.12.47.png
Pick an available Pancake subscription plan or create one customized for you
	•	Step 4: Once you’ve chosen your plan, click Create Subscription to proceed.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/edcbc893-32af-4d7a-af13-1d8ebaa9e562_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 09.15.18.png
Click "Create subscription" to create your Pancake package.
	•	Step 5: Enter electronic invoice information as required. Once completed, click Save and get QR code for payment.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/f26e09f4-7e91-460f-bff9-bb71f5dd32f1_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 09.20.47.png
Enter electronic invoice information
	•	Step 6: Make payment by scanning the QR code displayed on the screen. 
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/10/24/cd35474d-ca97-4095-b5ce-e62ed81f840f_Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 09.23.50.png
Pay for your Pancake subscription
- After your transfer is successfully processed, the system will activate your subscription within a few minutes. 
- If you entered incorrect information or your subscription isn't activated automatically, please contact us

Payment Successful
Once your subscription plan is activated, you can add staff members and platforms to start using Pancake.
