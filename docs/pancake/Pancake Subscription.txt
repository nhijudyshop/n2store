Pancake Subscription
Easily manage your Pancake experience with our subscription plans. Choose between a free trial or a paid subscription to get started. Once your trial ends, seamlessly upgrade to a paid subscription or create a new plan to continue enjoying Pancake’s powerful features.
Each subscription includes a set number of pages and staff accounts, tailored to your needs.
The price depends on the quantity of pages, staff members, and the duration you wish to use Pancake.

For example: If you need to oversee 4 Facebook pages, 5 Shopee pages, and 1 Lazada page, and have 10 staff members managing these pages, simply purchase a subscription for 10 pages and 10 staff members.
