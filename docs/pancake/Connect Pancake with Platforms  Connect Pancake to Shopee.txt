Guide to Connecting Pancake with Shopee
Requirement: 
- Before connecting Pancake with Shopee, you need to already have a Shopee Seller Account. 
- The connection will not work if you have not registered as a seller.
- You can sign up for a seller account directly on Shopee here.
1. Connect
✅ Step 1: On the Pancake homepage/ Dashboard → select Connect.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/9/11/99b7a209-148b-48ba-a823-5e65fdedb872_Screenshot 2025-09-11 at 17.57.37.png
✅ Step 2: Select the Shopee section
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/9/11/2deacb7e-a2d8-4b17-82b3-9e6bc7d28aa6_Screenshot 2025-09-11 at 17.57.52.png
✅ Step 3: Successful Connection
After a successful connection, Shopee will appear in the Dashboard.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/9/11/46c05e09-cc45-413e-979c-f118ca309c01_Screenshot 2025-09-11 at 17.58.04.png
Note: After Successfully Connecting Pancake with Shopee:
 - Users who have already connected with POS (Pancake’s order management system) will be redirected to the Conversations section. 
- The system will automatically pull messages and reviews from the past 2 weeks.
https://statics.pancake.vn/pancake-docs/2025/9/11/2dffc13a-6cf0-4770-8a91-28bd1e7fb516_Screenshot 2025-09-11 at 17.58.13.png
Note: 
- If the user has not connected with POS yet: (Connecting POS will help sync products and enable some POS features).
- Similar to the registration steps in Pancake (redirecting to Shopee and logging in), after connecting with the POS, the system will redirect to the Conversation page. 
- If you do not connect with POS, you can exit and manually go to the page from the Dashboard.
