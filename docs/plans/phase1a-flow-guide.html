Phase 1A — Huong Dan Su Dung So Sach Giao Hang Tung Phan

Flow chi tiet tu luc Duyen dat hang den khi Sale thay trang thai giao hang
0 Tong quan tinh nang

Van de: Truoc day, Duyen dat NCC 100 cai nhung NCC chi giao 60 truoc — Sale khong biet, cu tuong hang du de di don. Ket qua: chot don cho khach nhung khong co hang giao.

Giai phap: So Sach gio day cho phep ghi nhan giao hang tung phan (delivery batches). Khi NCC giao tung lo, Duyen nhap so luong vao — he thong tu tinh SL Cho giao, SL NCC huy va hien badge trang thai de Sale nhin la biet ngay.

4 trang thai giao hang:

Chua giao — NCC chua giao gi ca Tung phan — NCC da giao mot phan (VD: 60/100) Du — NCC da giao du so luong Huy — NCC huy khong giao

Ai dung gi:

Duyen (Dat hang) Nhap SL Duyen order, nhap lo giao hang, nhap SL NCC huy  |  Sale Xem bang, loc theo NCC / trang thai, biet hang nao da ve / dang cho

Giai thich cac cot:

  • SL Khach dat — Tu dong lay tu don hang TPOS (khong sua duoc)
  • SL Duyen order — Duyen nhap tay so luong da dat NCC. Sua duoc, tu dong sync 2 chieu voi Quan ly Order
  • CL K-D — Chenh lech Khach dat - Duyen order. +20 = khach dat nhieu hon Duyen order 20 cai
  • SL NCC giao — Tong NCC da giao. Sua truc tiep hoac nhan nut [+] de nhap tung lo
  • SL Cho giao — Tu dong tinh: Duyen order - NCC giao - NCC huy. 40 = con cho 40 cai
  • SL NCC huy — Duyen nhap khi NCC bao huy khong giao
  • CL D-N — Chenh lech Duyen order - NCC giao - NCC huy
  • TT Giao — Badge tu dong: Chua giao / Tung phan / Du / Huy
  • Ton kho — Tu Quan ly Order (QtyAvailable), co the chinh tay
  • Ghi chu — Ghi chu tu do

7 Lap lai cho den khi du hang

Moi lan NCC giao them, Duyen nhap lo moi → he thong tu cap nhat badge. Khi SL NCC giao + SL NCC huy >= SL Duyen order → badge chuyen Du.

Neu nhap bang batch form (cach B), toan bo lich su lo giao duoc luu lai. Click nut [+] se thay cac lo truoc do.

5 Logic tu dong tinh toan

Moi khi SL Duyen order, SL NCC giao, hoac SL NCC huy thay doi:

SL Cho giao = max(0, Duyen order - NCC giao - NCC huy)

TT Giao =
  Neu Duyen order = 0 → Chua giao
  Neu NCC huy >= Duyen order → Huy
  Neu NCC giao >= Duyen order - NCC huy → Du
  Neu NCC giao > 0 (nhung chua du) → Tung phan
  Con lai → Chua giao
Quan trong: Cac gia tri nay duoc tinh tu dong, nguoi dung khong can va khong the sua truc tiep "SL Cho giao" hay "TT Giao".
6 Vi du thuc te (Scenarios)

Scenario A: NCC giao du 1 lan

1Duyen dat NCC 50 cai ao B34C → nhap "SL Duyen order" = 50
2NCC giao du 50 cai → Duyen nhap "SL NCC giao" = 50
Ket qua: SL Cho giao = 0 | TT Giao = Du | Sale biet hang da du, co the chot don

Scenario B: NCC giao 2 lo

1Duyen dat 100 cai → "SL Duyen order" = 100 | TT Giao = Chua giao
2NCC giao lo 1: 60 cai → Click [+] nhap lo 60 | TT Giao = Tung phan | Cho giao = 40
3NCC giao lo 2: 40 cai → Click [+] nhap lo 40 | TT Giao = Du | Cho giao = 0
Ket qua: He thong tu dong cong 60 + 40 = 100. Sale thay badge xanh.

Scenario C: NCC huy 1 phan

1Duyen dat 80 cai | TT Giao = Chua giao
2NCC giao 50 cai | TT Giao = Tung phan | Cho giao = 30
3NCC bao huy 30 cai con lai → Duyen nhap "SL NCC huy" = 30
Ket qua: Cho giao = 80 - 50 - 30 = 0 | TT Giao = Du (vi da giao + huy = toan bo)

Scenario D: Sale loc tim hang dang cho

1Sale mo So Sach
2Chon bo loc "TT Giao" = "Tung phan"
3Chi hien cac SP ma NCC da giao mot phan → Sale biet SP nao can cho them
Ket qua: Sale chi thay nhung SP dang cho hang, de uu tien chot don cho khach co hang truoc.
